# Nori Promo Video Design

## Style Prompt

Create a refined Chinese product film for Nori, an AI Agent backend for Xiaohongshu-first account operations. The feeling is credible, warm, editorial, and operational: less "magic AI generator", more "calm content command center". Use full-screen product UI compositions, precise motion, soft paper texture, structured dashboards, flowing evidence lines, and XHS-inspired red as a restrained signal color. The viewer should feel that messy brand inputs are being turned into account positioning, market learning, export-ready content packages, human review, and measurable iteration.

## Colors

- Background paper: `#f8f3ea`
- Ink text: `#1f2723`
- Muted ink: `#657069`
- XHS signal red: `#e34848`
- Strategy green: `#2f7d58`
- Evidence amber: `#d7a647`
- Panel surface: `#fffaf2`
- Night ink: `#17211d`

## Typography

- Primary UI and titles: `PingFang SC`, `Hiragino Sans GB`, `Noto Sans CJK SC`, `Microsoft YaHei`, sans-serif
- Editorial accent: `Songti SC`, `STSong`, serif

## Motion

- Calm but decisive. Elements enter with 0.35-0.65s easing and slight depth.
- Lines and chips move like a production pipeline, not like decorative particles.
- Scene transitions use soft wipes, panel shifts, and product-state morphs.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not claim auto-publishing, automatic follower growth, full-platform operations, or guaranteed viral notes.
- Do not show one-prompt magic generation as the core story.
- Do not use generic blue-purple AI gradients, neon-dark dashboards, or empty glass cards.
- Do not overload the user with technical module names; translate modules into user-understandable product capabilities.
